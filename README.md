**1,000 questions · ~4,000 explanations · 20 practice exams.**

Each certification ships **four 50-question sets**:

- **Practice Exam 1 & 2** — definitional questions covering the exam blueprint, domain by domain.
- **Scenario Exam 1 & 2** — multi-constraint, harder questions written the way the real exam frames them, with the actual CLI command (`kubectl`, `terraform`, `aws`, `az`, `gcloud`, `gsutil`) in the explanation wherever it's instructive.

> The CKA set carries an honesty note in-app: the real exam is hands-on and performance-based. These questions sharpen the concepts behind the tasks — they don't replace practice on a live cluster.

## Why it's different

Most quiz sites tell you the answer. This one tells you **why** — and why the other three options are wrong. Every distractor has its own explanation, so you learn the boundary between concepts instead of memorizing the keyword that wins.

- **Wrong answers explained** — each option carries a reason, right and wrong alike.
- **Two modes** — *practice* checks instantly as you go; *exam* runs the clock and scores you per domain, against the real blueprint weighting.
- **Per-domain breakdown** — see exactly which domains carried you and which sank the score.
- **Drill your misses** — after a run, retry just the questions you got wrong.
- **Question of the day** — one fresh, unscored question per cert, daily, to keep the muscle warm.
- **Yours to keep** — scores save to your browser's local storage. No account, no server, no analytics.

## Accuracy & contributions

Every question is validated for answer-key integrity, no duplicate stems, and non-empty explanations before it ships. Still, blueprints shift and mistakes slip through.

Spot something wrong or out of date? Open an [issue](https://github.com/eknatha/certs/issues) with the question id (e.g. `aws-saa-c03-s3-q12`) and what's off. Corrections and new questions are welcome via pull request — match the schema in `data/SCHEMA.md` and keep every option's explanation filled in.
